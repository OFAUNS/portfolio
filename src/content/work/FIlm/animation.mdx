> The starting point of this project is to built upon elements rich in mythology: 
A bird, a church, and stained glass. A story is woven from these mythic elements, inviting vast imagination.

The bird becomes an explorer. This explorer could represent an individual, a group, or any form of life—or perhaps, just as it appears, it is simply a bird. The church symbolizes life's journey, and the different colored lights represent the various states and encounters within that life. These experiences impact the bird, symbolizing the changes and growth of life.
